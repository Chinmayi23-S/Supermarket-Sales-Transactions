# Supermarket-Sales-Transactions
Interpreting measure of central tendency and using multiple regression analysis to check dependency of previous 2 years sales on 2023 Sales.


Skills Showed:
#ExcelDataAnalysisTool
#StatisticalAnalysis
#InterpretatingModel
#RegressionAnalysis

Key Insights
The least revenue generating country is Canada ($11,067)
Average revenue of each city and product family is identified using Excel Pivot.
R-squared value = 0.2724 Therefore, 27.24% of the variance in the 2023 sales is explained by the model.
Significance: F = 0.239 (p value) which is more than 0.05. So, the model is not statistically significant.

p-value of ‘2021 Sales’ = 0.1034
p-value of ‘2022 Sales’ = 0.9704
The p-values of the 2021 sales and 2022 sales are significantly greater than 0.05. 
There is not enough evidence that 2023 Sales depend on previous years sales. 
The model as whole is statistically not significant.
